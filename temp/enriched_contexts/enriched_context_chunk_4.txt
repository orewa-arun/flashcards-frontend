## DEFINITIONS (Use for `concise` answers)
- **L'audace (Audacity)**: According to the slide, audacity is a quality to be cultivated at all ages. It involves believing in oneself, launching initiatives, innovating, learning from failures, recovering and starting again. It also entails engaging with and for others, acting as a responsible, respectful, and caring manager, and positively impacting society. It is presented as a force for personal growth, broadening horizons, and fueling imagination to transform the world.
